Welcome to Aurora!

Welcome to our Discord server, dedicated to Roleplaying (RP) in Garry's Mod! Find a friendly community of like-minded players who love immersing themselves in worlds and scenarios specifically designed for RP situations.

Our server hosts regular RP sessions with diverse plots and scenarios. From the everyday life of a small town, brimming with new events, to thrilling adventures in a post-apocalyptic world, there's something for everyone!

We foster a friendly and respectful atmosphere. Every member is encouraged to contribute ideas for the next session or participate in co-creating scenarios with other players.

Join us for engaging roleplaying, make new friends, and create unforgettable moments within the Garry's Mod world!
